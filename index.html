Archimedes (IQ BackOffice Portal)

Archimedes is invoice approval system

Submit Invoices

Any employee can submit invoices. Login is not required to submit invoices.

  • Option 1: Send invoices to gemsupload@iqbackoffice.com. Invoices automatically uploads into Archimedes.
    • Attachments must be a pdf or word file
    • One full invoice attached as a pdf per email
    • No text in email is uploaded
    • No special characters in the pdf file name

  • Option 2: Fax the invoice to 818-325-8881 or email to IQ BackOffice team at gemsap@iqbackoffice.com to process manually.
To Approve Invoices
  • Login page: https://archimedes.iqbackoffice.com
  • Only invoice approvers will have user account. Username format starts with first initial and lastname. eg. jsmith.gems for the user John Smith

Review GL code and cost distribution before approving the invoice, if not reject it with comments

  • First icon (Approve):
    • If invoice is less than or equal to $1000 - Approve by choosing send to Return to IQ
    • If invoice is above $1000 - Approve by choosing send to: Your Next Approver
      • For all GWA and EY department heads, next approver is Head of School
      • For Head of School, next approver is Controller
      • For NYC department heads, next approver is CFO

  • Second icon (Reject): To reject invoice, Reject by choosing send to : Return to IQ (Commenst must be added for rejecting invoice)

  • Third icon (Forward without Approval): If invoice does not belong to your department, forward invoice for the right approver. Choose the person in Send to pick list and forward without approval.

For questions and technical support reach out to itsupport@gemsamericas.com
